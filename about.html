About Us

We are a picture Frame Business called “We Frame U” that started in a small suburb in Detroit Michigan in 2012, This is where our main office currently resides, we also have two sub-supporting offices one in Sacramento, California and the other one in the great city of New Orleans, Louisiana.

We pride our company with a 100% customer satisfaction guarantee are motto is “If our customers aren’t satisfied we aren’t satisfied either” We offer a wide array of different types of quality picture frames at the lowest possible cost.

All are picture frames are made right here in America, which attributes to our high quality of standards from American made materials and craftsmanship. We are also ISO 9001 registered and certified, which is a strict quality organization that sets international standards to ensure businesses demonstrate the ability to consistently meet customer and regulatory requirements.

In the past six years, our business has grown at an exponential rate with the expanding office in Sacramento in 2014, to our newest location in New Orleans in 2016.

We attribute our success to our great relations to all business aspects from customers, employees and vendors or any stakeholder vital to our success.
